Great, the Gerrit extension is working! Here are some more pro tips for using this extension:

  • You can use keyboard shortcuts! From a Gerrit email, press "w" to open the change in Gerrit, and "W" to approve the change.
  • When you're looking at colored diffs of a change, you can double-click on a green line to add a comment.
  • If you have a chatty bot regularly commenting on your changes (like Jenkins, etc.), you can add their user names to the Bot names field on the Options page, and the extension will do a better job deriving change status and showing you diffs between patch sets.
